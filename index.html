Good Harbor Beach

Good Harbor Beach is located in Gloucester, MA and is about a 45 minute drive from Boston. This beach is perfect for families and people of all ages. A small resturant, italian ice stands, and restrooms are located on the beach for your convience. From crystal clear water to big waves, Good Harbor Beach is one of MA's best beaches.
